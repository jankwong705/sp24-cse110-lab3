Unfinished Business from Last Meeting:

Although all systems within the Republic have been surveyed, General Grievous' whereabouts are still unclear. He is presumably hiding in the Outer Rims.
Search on the outlying systems are needed.

New Business from This Meeting:

The droid attacks on the Wookies is concerning. Since Kashyyyk is a system we cannot afford to lose, forces will need to be sent to assist the Wookies.
Master Yoda agreed to head to Kashyyyk to assist them.

He has good relations with the Wookies.

Miscellaneous:

Anakin Skywalker feels discontent towards not being granted the rank of master despite being offered a seat on the Jedi Council.
His overly close relationship with the Chancellor also raises concerns.
